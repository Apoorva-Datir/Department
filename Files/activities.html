Two Days workshop on Big Data Hadoop for Faculties

PDC Cell of computer Department has organized 2 days workshop on "Big Data Hadoop" for Faculties. This was organized with an objective of to learn big data analytics & processing using Hadoop. Resourse person was Mr.Yogesh Pawar from Suman Technology. This event was successfully coordinated by Mr.Ganesh Deshmukh,Mr.Rajesh Lomte and Mr.Vishal Wagh.

Big Data Hadoop Workshop

Computer Engineering Department under PDC has organized two days workshop on Big Data and Hadoop on 27th to 28th June 2015 and 5 th to 6 th Sep 2015 . The trainer for the workshop was Mr. Manish Jain and Mr. Rahul Gaikwad Techbharat in association with ACT IIT Kharagpur.

Add-on Course : Developer Training For Apache Big Data Hadoop

Computer Engineering Department under PDC conducted Add-on course on "Developer Training For Apache Big Data Hadoop" for third year students from 30/11/2015 to 05/12/2015. Total 28 students have completed the training.The trainer for this course was Mr.Yogesh Pawar from Suman Technology. Mr. Yogesh Pawar is Senior Big Data Architect at Suman Technology.he has 15+ years experience in the IT industry . he also have strong expertise in enterprise applications design and development, framework development, code refactoring, re- engineering, legacy modernization, tools.

Add-on Course : MSITA(.Net, C#)

Computer Engineering Department under PDC conducted Add-on course on MSITA(.Net,c#) for third year students from 02/12/2015 to 11/12/2015. Total 74 students have completed the training.This training helps the students to learn and understand .net and c# programming with microsoft certification.The trainer for this course was Mr.Stevan and Mr. Omprakash Shukla from ATS Infotech Pvt.Ltd. Pune.

5 days workshop on Cisco Networking Routing and Switching

Computer Engineering Department under PDC has organized 5 days workshop on Cisco Networking routing and switching from 12/03/2106 to 16/03/2016 . Total 28 Students from Computer,E&TC and IT department has participated in this worshop.The trainer for the workshop was Mr. Mohmad Shariff.The purpose of training was to learn networking fundamentals ,Routing protocols and its analysis.
