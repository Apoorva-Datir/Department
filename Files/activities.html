Add-on Course : Developer Training For Apache Big Data Hadoop

Computer Engineering Department under PDC conducted Add-on course on "Developer Training For Apache Big Data Hadoop" for third year students from 30/11/2015 to 05/12/2015. Total 28 students have completed the training.The trainer for this course was Mr.Yogesh Pawar from Suman Technology. Mr. Yogesh Pawar is Senior Big Data Architect at Suman Technology.he has 15+ years experience in the IT industry . he also have strong expertise in enterprise applications design and development, framework development, code refactoring, re- engineering, legacy modernization, tools.

A Seminar on Formal Business Communication

A Seminar on 'Formal Business Communication' was organized by the CPDC cell of Computer Department for S.E, T.E and B.E students of Computer Department on 3rd February 2016. A Seminar on Presentation Skills and Grooming

The seminar was an articulation of a set of stories based on Public Speaking and successfully making a connection with the audience, Do's and Don'ts of Public Speaking, Presentation Skills (with or without PPTs) and Grooming Skills.
